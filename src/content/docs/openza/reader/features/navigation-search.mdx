title: Navigation and Search
description: Use views, headings, search, zoom, and automatic reload
---

Openza Reader keeps the reading surface quiet while making larger Markdown files easy to navigate.

## View modes

Use the **View** menu to switch between Preview, Raw, and Side by side. Preview is the default formatted view. Raw shows selectable, read-only Markdown. Side by side shows the original text and formatted preview together.

## Table of contents

Reader builds the table of contents from document headings. Show or hide it from the toolbar in Preview and Side by side views. It stays hidden in Raw view.

## Search

Select **Search** or press `Ctrl+F`, then enter the text you want to find.

## Zoom

Use zoom to make the document easier to read on different screens or during a presentation.

## Automatic reload

When another app changes the open Markdown file, Reader reloads it after a brief pause and tries to keep your approximate reading position.
