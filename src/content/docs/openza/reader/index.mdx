## What you can do

- **Open Markdown files.** Open `.md` and `.markdown` files from the app, File Explorer, or drag and drop.
- **Choose a reading view.** Switch between Preview, Raw, and Side by side read-only views.
- **Find your way around.** Use headings, search, recent files, zoom, and reader themes.
- **Control remote content.** Choose whether remote images load and open external links in your browser.
